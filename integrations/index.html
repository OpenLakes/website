How it works

Get your data flowing in minutes, not months.

1. Choose your source

Search our catalog of 500+ data sources. Whether it's a production database, a SaaS app, or files in cloud storage, we've got you covered.

2. Enter credentials

Provide read-only access to your data source. OpenLakes never modifies your source systems. All credentials are encrypted at rest.

3. Set your schedule

Choose real-time sync for databases or set a schedule that works for batch sources. Daily, hourly, or custom cron expressions.

4. Query in SQL

Your data lands in Apache Iceberg tables. Query it with Trino, transform it with Spark, or visualize it in Superset. All from one platform.
